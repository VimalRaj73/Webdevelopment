The Tale of Naruto Uzumaki

Naruto Classic : A little notorious boy named Naruto had a Nine tailed fox demon trapped inside of him, and his whole village feared him. He gets a team of other ninja called Team7, and tries to ascend to the next ninja rank. His friend Sasuke gets a curse mark from a bad guy named Orochimaru, and ends up leaving the village to kill his brother Itachi. Naruto tries to stop Sasuke, and he failed to bring him back and got depressed.

Naruto Shippuden : He leaves his village for almost three years,for training ninjutsu with a Pervy sage named Jiraiya. In search of jinchuiriki, the Akatsuki catches all nine tailed demons except Naruto's and Octopops. A Uchiha named Obito,who is a member of Akatsuki started a Fourth world war on 5 great nations. Naruto teaming up with Sasuke Uchiha and with the help of Allied shinobi force, he defeated Madara Uchiha. Later he become the Hokage of the Konoha Village.
